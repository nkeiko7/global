We are all givers.

If the feeling is there, anything exchanged between two people is a gift.
That includes "good morning", "thank you" and "I'm sorry". Encouragement, consideration and sharing, too.

Express your feelings through words, show your affection with your voice and facial expressions and sometimes with a present.

Everyone is a giver from the day he's born.
MUJI is delighted to show its support for those lovable people.
